Who We Are

A Global Standard in Chemical Engineering

Since our establishment in 2001, ChemManufacture has grown from a local research lab into a major multi-national manufacturer. We process petrochemical feedstocks, formulate bio-based agriculture agents, and synthesize specialty polymers for automotive and industrial clients.

We operate 12 advanced manufacturing plants globally, running automated batch synthesis and purification systems. Our central mission is to pioneer highly active molecular solutions while leading the industry in environmental responsibility, striving for low-waste solvent recycles and zero carbon footprints.

Our Journey

Milestones in Chemical Innovation

2001

Founding & Small Lab Setup

ChemManufacture was founded as a private research laboratory in Houston, TX, focusing on high-purity solvent purification and fine chemical R&D for local pharmaceutical clients.

2007

Industrial Scale-Up & Petrochemical Entry

Commissioned our first multi-ton batch reactor and expanded operations to petrochemical cracking intermediates. Partnered with major energy companies across North America.

2013

Global Distribution & ISO Audits

Earned international ISO 9001:2015 credentials. Established secure distribution channels in Europe and Asia, operating specialized tanker shipping networks.

2019

Green Catalyst Research & Biodegradables

Inaugurated our central Green Catalysis R&D Lab. Formulated zeolite-based copper catalysts that significantly cut industrial NOx emissions, receiving global green awards.

2026

Fully Automated Zero-Liquid Plants

Integrated state-of-the-art flow automation and wastewater bioreactors at all 12 facilities, achieving absolute zero-liquid discharge and setting industrial benchmarks.
