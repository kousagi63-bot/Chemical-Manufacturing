What We Excel In

Comprehensive Chemical Manufacturing Solutions

Petrochemical Refining

Processing raw hydrocarbons into chemical building blocks. Includes fractional distillation, catalytic cracking, and gas scrubbing to produce pure ethylene, propylene, and aromatic intermediates under rigorous temperature tracking.

APPLICATIONS: Fuels, synthetic fibers, chemical solvents, plastic feedstocks.

Polymers & Resins

Synthesizing heavy macromolecules, acrylic resins, and advanced thermoplastics. We customize molecular weight distribution (MWD) to achieve superior mechanical stiffness, flame retardance, and thermal structural durability.

APPLICATIONS: Auto components, electronic casings, aerospace alloys, protective sealants.

Specialty Fine Chemicals

Providing specialized custom chemical synthesis at laboratory to pilot batch scale. We supply high-purity organic reagents, pharmaceutical building blocks, and custom dyes verified by chromatography purity profiles.

APPLICATIONS: Drug synthesis, food preservation, chemical reagents, custom research.

Agricultural Chemistry

Engineering crop-enhancing agents, biodegradable pesticide compounds, and biological soil nutrients. Our formulations prioritize chemical stability and low environmental toxic runoff into water channels.

APPLICATIONS: Grain crop farming, weed control, fertilizer emulsions, soil enrichment.

Catalyst R&D

Designing zeolites, copper nanoparticles, and bio-catalytic systems. Our custom catalysts lower operational activation energies, accelerating industrial reactions while cutting hydrocarbon emissions.

APPLICATIONS: Boiler emission control, catalytic cracking, synthetic speeds, waste reduction.

Quality & Wastewater Treatment

Running high-pressure purification systems for solvents, alongside advanced chemical wastewater treatment. We deploy ceramic nano-filtration membranes to ensure Zero-Liquid Discharge compliance.

APPLICATIONS: Effluent treatment, solvent recycling, clean water recycling, lab diagnostics.
